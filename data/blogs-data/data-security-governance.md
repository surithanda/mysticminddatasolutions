### Get to Know the Project – Overview & Highlights

Strong governance is essential as businesses navigate stricter data regulations. This project explores governance frameworks ensuring security, compliance, and trust.

Key highlights include:

- **Data cataloging & classification** to organize information.  
- **Access controls** safeguarding sensitive assets.  
- **Compliance with GDPR & CCPA** requirements.  
- **Data lineage tracking** for audit readiness.  

### Building Strategies for the Future

To strengthen governance:

1. **Adopt Governance Tools:** Leverage cloud-native compliance frameworks.  
2. **Implement Access Policies:** Protect sensitive datasets.  
3. **Monitor Continuously:** Track data usage and anomalies.
